Maven Toys 2024 Airport Location Expansion Plan

Project Details:

Maven Toys is a fictitious chain of toy stores; they currently have 50 stores spanning 29 cities throughout Mexico. They hired me as a BI consultant to analyze patterns and trends in their sales and inventory data in order to create an expansion plan for 2024. I also sourced Mexican airport passenger traffic in order to determine which airports were best suited for expansion. Data cleaning was conducted in Excel, exploration in SQL and visualization in Tableau.

To view the full interactive Tableau Dashboard click Here

This analyis will answer the following important questions:
  • Where should Maven Toys expand to?
  • How many stores should they open?
  • Which products will perform best at the new location(s)?

Insights:

  • Where should Maven Toys expand to?
  • Mavenâ€™s top 2 stores are located at Mexico City and Guadalajara International Airports, which account for 7% of total sales. And overall airport locations do better.
  • Cancun and Tijuana international airports are top choices for expansion in 2024.
  • On average 2.6 stores have been opened per year.

Recommendations:

  • Open two Stores: Cancun and Tijuana International Airports are both fast growing, and very busy airports based on passenger traffic. These would work well since existing airport stores are top performers.
  • Display Top Products: Since space is at a premium at airport stores only stock products with above average sales within each category (based on top performers from existing airport stores):
  • Toys - Lego Bricks, Action Figure, Animal Figures
  • Arts & Crafts - Majic Sand, Kids Makeup Kit
  • Electronics - Colorbuds, Gamer Headphones
  • Games - Glass Marbles, Rubik's Cube, Deck of Cards
  • Sports & Outdoors - Nerf gun, Splash Balls, Dart Gun

  • Increase Sales: Colorbuds has been steadily decreasing in sales over time. This could be due to a variety of factors which would need further investigation. Meanwhile, Colorbuds should be displayed prominently in stores to see if sales improve.
  • Increase Profitability: Lego Bricks is the top seller but has among the lowest profit ratio of all the products. Since it's becoming a very popular product consider raising the price, avoiding markdowns and working with suppliers to reduce product costs.

Data Limitations:

  • The Maven Toys data is not comprehensive as it only covers one full year (2022) and partial data for 2023 (up to September).
  • Airport passenger traffic data is only available for 2022.
  • To better analyze current trends, obtaining updated Maven Toys data beyond September 2023 and acquiring 2023 airport passenger traffic data would be necessary.
